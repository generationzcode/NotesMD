### Terms in underdamped systems
- overshoot - ratio A/B where A is the maximum by which the response exceeds it's ultimate value and B is the ultimate value of the response. Used in underdamped responses
- ![[Pasted image 20250206172757.png]]
- Decay ratio - C/A, basically the ratio of the amounts above the ultimate value for two successive peaks
- ![[Pasted image 20250206172850.png]]
- Period of oscillation:
- ![[Pasted image 20250206172941.png]]
- natural period of oscillation is just $\omega=1/\tau$
- Response time - time taken to reach ultimate value and stays there
- Rise time - time taken to reach ultimate value for the first time
