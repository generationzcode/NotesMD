## Process
- Preform (dry reinforcement fabric) placed inside mold
- mold is clamped shut under pressure
- low viscosity liquid resin like epoxy is pumped into mold
- resin cures
- mold opens, part removed
## Uses
> Why use this? why not put a preform in a regular injection molding apparatus?

- Screw somehow shears the fibers, making them shorter and less effective at reinforcement
