## mRNA
- Average length of mRAN is 1100 units
- each ribosome can bind every 50 nt on mRNA
- Maximum number of ribosomes per mRNA is 1100/50 = 22 working simultaneously
- the rate of animo acid translation is 16 amino acids per second
- The number of translations of all 22 ribosomes are working is 22x16 = 352
- If the average length of a protein is 367 AA, then effective translation is 1 protein per second
- The average life for an mRNA is 6 minutes
- mean lifetime is 519 seconds
- So 1 messenger RNA will give rise to 519 proteins per lifetime
