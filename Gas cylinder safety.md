#safety 
> Gases can kill you sometimes

## Types of gases
* Flammable gases
* Inert gas
* Oxidising gas
* Pyrophoric gas - spontaneousl ignites in presence of gas
* Corrosive gas - burn and destroy body tissues
* Poisonous gas - harmful to humans after a certain concentration
### Examples
* Inert gases - Argon, helium
* Flammable - Methane, HYdrogen
* Toxic - CO, SO2, phosgene
* Pyrophoric gases - Silane, Phosphine
* Oxidisers - oxygen, nitrous oxide
## Oxygen levels
* 19.5 is the minimum level of oxygen in the air
* If it goes below this, you'll die of brain rot, no breathing, fainting
* excess oxygen is a fire hazard
##  flammable gases
* Leakage causes flammable vapour air mixture
* Flammability limits are what show percent of vapour in air which causes DANGER
* LFL - lower flammability limit, UFL - upper flammability limit

| Gas      | LFL  | UFL |
| -------- | ---- | --- |
| Ethylene | 2.7% | 36% |
|          |      |     |
## Toxic gases
* Toxicity is the ability of a gas to cause unwanted physical issues
* The concentration of an airborne substance to which an average person can be exposed to repeatedly without adverse effects - TLV - threshold limit value

| Gas             | TLV      |
| --------------- | -------- |
| Carbon Monoxide | 29mg/m3  |
| Phosgene        | 0.4mg/m3 |
