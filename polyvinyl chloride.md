* Rigid packaging, pipes
* Acetylene gas and HCl **or** ethylene and Cl2 to manufacture the monomer then polymerized by suspension or emulsion. Mostly PVC suspension grade resins are used
* Higher [[K - value]] when higher will be the molecular weight. Emulsion gives higher K value
* Higher K value are used in calendaring, normal used in PVC pipes and injection grade used in pipe fittings and such
* native state, without any additives, PVC is cannot be processed because before melting, the PVC will fuse together because of chlorine
* additives added - heat stabilizer, lubricant between the wall and the polymer
* the maximum additives you usually add is about 30 PHR (parts per hundred resin)
* To flexiblize something you add a plasticizer
* the first few plasticizers were pthalates - now banned because they cause cancer. Still used in certain applications
* now we use oils and all
* density is 1.35 -1.4 g/cm3
