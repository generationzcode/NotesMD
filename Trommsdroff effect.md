- Suppose the initiator rate is more than the termination rate, the system become unsteady and viscosity builds up and causes the molecular weight to build up
- Trommsdroff figured out that this is an autoaccelerating phenomenon and eventually leads to gelling
- "when rate of initiation is more than the rate of termination, gelling occurs"
- This is seen a lot in [[Bulk Polymerization]], since there's no medium
