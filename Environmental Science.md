* clogged filters were a big problem
* alum was introduced as a water treatment technique in the 18th century and widely used in the 19th century
* chlorination became common in the 20th century
* Now people are concerned about adverse effects of chlorination, so work into potassium permanganate is being done

## Ecology 
> Ecosystems are composed of interacting living organisms and their physical environment

* populations generally maintain a balance state (homeostasis)
* there is a dynamic equilibrium which is whats behind homeostasis with stabilizing (for negative feedback) and destabilizing (for positive feedback) effects. This maintains the population in a balanced state
