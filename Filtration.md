>  A removal of solids from a fluid by passing through a filter medium (septum) on which the solids are deposited

* Carried out to recover solid/fluid or none of them or both
* Covers range of solid content from traces to a high percentage
* Driving force is the pressure difference
* [[Types of filters]]
* [[Mechanism of filtration]] 
* [[Constant pressure filtration]]
* [[Constant rate filtration]]
* [[Filter aid]]
* Filter media - must retain solids to be filtered, must not plug/blind, must be resistant chemically and mechanically to survive the process conditions, must be affordable and allow cake to be cleaned off
* Cake deliquoring, dewatering - carried out to further reduce the moisture content of the filter cake. Done by pushing or pulling the air  (vacuum) or mechanical squeezing
* Cake dewatering improves the cake discharge and handling, reducing the transportation costs and removes the contaminated liquids from the cake
