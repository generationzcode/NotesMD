## Principles of filtration
* Feed slurry to a filter with a pressure difference due to gravity
* Initially there is no filter cake, over time the filter cake is developed
* The slurry passes through the filter and is removed as the filtrate
* Filtration is a special case of flow through porous media
* There are two resistances in filtration which the filtrate media and the cake resistance in the pressure drop. The cake resistance becomes the controlling resistance
* We modify the flow through porous media equations for filtration for this reason
* With time either the flow rate decreases or the pressure drop increases
* This gives rise to two types of filtration -

* [[Constant rate filtration]]
