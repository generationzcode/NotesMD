### Linear step growth
* Happens when carboxylic acid or acid halide reacts with alcohol group
* Reaction between the carboxylic acid and the alcohol groups is slow and has to be done in the presence of an acid catalyst such as sulfuric acid, at 80-300C
* Almost always RA2+RB2, sometimes ARB
