* [[polyolefins]] - [[polypropylene]], [[polyethylene]]
* thermo plastics can be classified in three types - commodity, specialty and engineering
* in commodity thermoplastics you have polyolefins, further classified into two types - polyethylene and polypropylene.
* Polyethylene- linear low density polyethylene(LLDPE), low density polyethylene(LDPE), high density polyethylene(HDPE), high molecular weight polyethylene (HMWP)
* Polypropylene- homopolymer polypropylene,copolymer of polypropylene
* vinyl polymers - CH2=CHX - polystyrene, poly vinyl chloride, polyacrylonitriles, poly vinyl difluoride
* these have been separated based on the thermal properties and density
