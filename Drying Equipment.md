## Tray dryers
- These are made for drying solids or pastes
- They are used when the production is small
- They have two "trucks" with racks that contain a number of trays where the solids or paste are kept to dry
- These require a lot of labour for loading and unloading

## Screen Conveyor Dryers
- ![[Pasted image 20250408191503.png]]
- The above image is barely visible but it explains the basic concept
- Less labour in general needed here
- usually 2 metres wide and 4-50 metres long
- The screen is permeable so air can pass from below as well
- These handle coarse granular, flaky or fibrous substances well, but fine powdery pastes need to be preformed before to avoid dusting up the whole thing

## Tower Dryers
![[Pasted image 20250408191925.png]]
- There are turbines for air flow and trays rotate and have material scraped off them and fall to the trays below to be scraped and keep falling till the product is dried and discharged
