> Prolog used here

- You got facts, queries and rules. Queries are like facts and rules except, with an unknown in them and a question mark at the end. A dot at the end shows that it's a factor a rule
- The program evaluates queries by trying to look for a resolution refutation of the negated query - basically an example of the query not not being true
- It goes through this by looping over all the facts it has and changing stuff using "chronological backtracking", just normal looping literally
