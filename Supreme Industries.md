### Plastic Piping Systems
* Aqua gold is for potable drinking water, no lead, cheaper, welding is more economical, similar performance to metal pipes.Lightweight.Less corrosion compared to GI(galvanized iron) pipes. Good number of pipe fittings compatible with the system. For a time where most pipes are GI, these can come in clutch for new buildings and all[details](https://www.supreme.co.in/pipe/products/aquagold-upvc-high-pressure-plumbing-system)
* UPVC high pressure pipes - can take a higher pressure than aqua gold and have all the advantages of aqua gold but fewer fittings, since it's only to be used in specialized areas
* Lifeline hot, cold water pipes. Pricier than Aqua gold but better insulation. MOre cost effective than GI in the long run
* PPR pipes - better than the previous 3 in every aspect just more expensive
* PP - R pipes - rugged use and low sound
* Solvent to "weld" pvc - SILBOND
* SILAID lubricant for jointing of pipes
* Thread sealant - BLUESIL
* ELITE advance plumbing system - radial heating of floor, high pressure uses with low insulation requirements possibly (ask someone about this)
* ECOLINE for reclaimed water - cheaper, worse quality compared to aqua gold and the rest. This is for non - potable water for gardening, toilets, etc
* ELECTRO FUSION fittings - for compressed gases, water at high pressures, pipe fittings with better welds and longer service life
