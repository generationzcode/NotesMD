## Federal System
- Federal or unitary government
- Unitary government is when all the power is at the central - national government
- Federal government is when the power is divided into states and center and they each have their jurisdiction
- India's a federal government (kinda, some people call it quasi federal because the tilt of power favours central government)
### Center state relations
- this is further divided into legislative, administrative and financial relations
- parliament makes laws for anyone and everyone in india or outside (extra territorial)
- state makes laws only for itself
