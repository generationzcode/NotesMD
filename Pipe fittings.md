* Other approach is the head coefficient approach
* $$K.E = \rho V_{av}^2/2$$
* $$\Delta P = \frac{4fL}{D}*\rho v_{av}^2/2$$
* The first part is the head loss coefficient and is denoted by K:
* $$\Delta P = K*K.E$$
* This is called number of velocity head approach
