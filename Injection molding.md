- Used to process thermoplastics
- ABS, PP, PS, PE, Nylon, PC
## Process
- Two molds, as shown are kept together tightly, as molten polymer is injected in from the screw at high pressure (clamping and injection)
- Polymer solidifies in the mold (cooling)
- Molds separate, ejector pins push out the product (Ejection)
- This usually takes up to a minute per product
## Advantages
- high production speed
- precision
- low labor costs
- wider range of geometries possible
