> [[Surface vs interface]] has info on how surfaces are different to interfaces

- At an interface, massive changes in physical properties occur at molecular distances
- The energy at the surface is more than the energy at the bulk since it interacts with fewer other molecules at the surface
- The net force at the interface isn't 0, since the molecule isn't pulled equally by all sides
