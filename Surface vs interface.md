* A phase is anything that is continuous and homogeneous within a boundary
* Outside that boundary, there is another phase
* When dealing with condensed phases, the boundary between them is interfacial and between a condensed phase and vapour phase is a surface
* Molecules at the surface has more energy, thus keeps trying to adsorb
> question - why do molecules surrounded by fewer atoms have less energy?

* surface tension is force per unit length, tangential
* surface tension and surface free energy are the same numerically and dimensionally
* intensive properties dont depend on sizing but extensive properties do
