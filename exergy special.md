>[!most important note in thermo]
>All quantities like entropy and hemholtz energy, gibbs free energy, exergy, etc are conceptual quantities that are made up for us to evaluate systems and understand what is possible and what is not. Maxwell's relations are used to express relations between conceptual quantity and real quantities like volume, pressure and temperature
