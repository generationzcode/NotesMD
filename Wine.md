### Aroma of Wine
- monoterpene found in wine are glucosides(?) of linalool, nerol, geranol, and alpha terpanol
- The aroma of wine is released only after hydrolysis of glucosides
- Natural hydrolysis of glycodisically bound terpenes in acidic conditions of wine takes years, because beta glucosidases in wine are in conditions far from optimum
- natural grape enzymes usually cannot withstand the conditions of wine making
