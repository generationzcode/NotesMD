* The general form of the transfer function of a second order system is:$$G(s) = \frac{K_pf(t)}{\tau^2s^2+2C\tau s+1}$$
* When C>1, its overdamped, C=1 critically damped and C<1 underdamped
* There are many ways to get to second order processes - multicapacity processes (two first order connected in series), inherently second order (oscillations), process control caused
* Here tau is no longer the time period, now it's the natural period of oscillation of the system
