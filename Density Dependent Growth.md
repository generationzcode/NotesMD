## Beverton Holt Stock Recruitment Curve
- $$\frac{N_{t+1}}{N_t} = \frac{R_0}{1+[(R_0-1)/K]N_t}$$
- $$N_{t+1} = N_t\frac{R_0}{1+[(R_0-1)/K]N_t}$$
- It's a monotonically increasing hyperbolic mapping. (What does this mean??)
