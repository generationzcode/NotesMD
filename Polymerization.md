## Types of Polymerization

* The most basic requirement of polymerization is that each monomer must have a functionality greater than 2
* Functionality is the number of bonds it can make with other functional groups of other monomers
* Carothers classified polymerization reactions into **addition polymerization** and **condensation polymerization**. This classification isn't in use anymore as there was some overlap between the two
* The method of classifying polymerization reactions is in **[[step growth]]** and **[[chain growth]]** polymerizations
* Step growth polymerizations are characterized by a high rate of reaction at the start, levelling out later. For these polymerizations, high extent of reactions (of the functional groups) are required for high chain lengths
* Chain growth polymerizations have a constant rate of reaction from start to end 
>[!important]
>Polymers don't have a boiling point because of how large their molecular mass is and because they degrade before they can boil
