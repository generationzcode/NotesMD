## Column
* Usually contains a number of perforated plates, called trays stacked
* Columns with such plates are called sieve-plate columns

## Reboiler
* It's at the bottom of the column 
* Vapour formed here is richer in the substance with the lower boiling point than the bottom product that is unvapourized
## Condenser
* The overhead product must be condensed from vapour to liquid
* The output of the condenser is divided between the distillate and the reflux
