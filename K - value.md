> K value is mostly used to determine the polyvinyl chloride molecular weight

* Related to viscosity and molecular weight
* for understanding PVC you need to know K value
* when the k value is between 57 to 60, you get injection grade PVC, 60 to 67, extruding; 67 to 70, calendaring
* usually melt flow index is used, but since PVC doesnt melt, you need to determine the k value
* something related to the U tube manometer
