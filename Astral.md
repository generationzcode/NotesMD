## Historical introduction
* These guys started in 1998, Santej Gujarat. They made CPVC pipes after licensing from some US company
* They started uPVC manufacture from 2004 for water pipes. The first lead free kind
* Dholka plant made to manufacture more pipes and other stuff like solvent cement
* 2013 they began a plant in the south to gain market control there
* 2015 silencio piping (quiet pipes)
* 2020 forth they became a more general company, manufacturing water tanks as well
>[!TLDR]
>They licensed tech from goodrich to make pipes right when the GI transition was occuring, once that started, they licensed more stuff from goodrich on adhesives and all. They've entered the water tank business now, but I'm clueless as to what trend thats following, same with paints

## Piping
* CPVC pro - a proprietary CPVC formulation. Used in buildings to transport water, drinking grade. Rugged, small thermal coefficients
* Pex A pro - uses cross linked polyethylene for strength and flexibility (stronger than a plastic bag, weaker than a CPVC pipe), drinking grade, non toxic, used in awkward piping solutions, [[Supreme Industries]] doesn't have this
* Foamcore solution - Better load distribution, lighter weight, strong. Made up of two rigid layers with foam in between.
* multipex - crosslined PE, with aluminium middle layer. Good flexibility and strength, frost resistance, etc. 3 layers. Possibly a unique product
* PVC underground pipes, cheap, non drinking grade for sewage
* aquarius, eco pro - first is for drinking water using lead free uPVC and second for reclaimed water
* Silencio for low noise application
* Drain pro - 3 layer drain pipe for strength. PP is mineral filled apparently. Fittings have a depth gauge
* manhole inspection chambers and lids
* Their cross linked polyethylene seems unique
## Adhesives

Sell polymer based adhesives for maintenance, woodcare, leakage, seapage, cracks solution, waterproofing adhesives (only found these guys selling this...), admixtures, mortars for tile and levelling
gap filling adhesives (work on expansion joints as well), tapes, plumbing specific formulations for sealing and cementing, sealants, putties for use in cars, for product application
