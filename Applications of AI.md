## Image Recognition
- How computers can get high level understanding from an image or video
- Sub domains - object and event detection, video tracking, motion analysis, scene reconstruction, image restoration
- typical phases of an image recognition system: image acquisition,preprocessing, feature extraction, segmentation, high level processing, decision making
## Speech Recognition
- Spoken language to readable text
- Speaker independent systems - work on any speaker
- Speaker dependent systems - only work on one personalized voice, can be used as authentication as well
## Datamining
- extracting actionable patterns from large datasets
- Used in businesses to predict inventory, sales, etc.

## Adaptive Control
- controller that acts in a system with parameters that are uncertain and varying
- The controller must adapt with time
