- These are polyethers
- contain epoxidegroups - two carbons one oxygen in that triangle like structure
- Bisphenol A diglycidyl ether is widely used, this is the reaction shown above. It is a reactin of bisphenol A and epichlorohydrin
## Curing
- They require a curing agent to open the epoxide rings and make a 3D network
- These curing agents are amines - DETA, TETA (diethylenetriamine and tri...), phenolic compounds, etc
## Properties
- Brittle
- slow curing
- toxic
- low shrinkage on cure
- high strength of adhesion
- chemical resistance
- Used in adhesives, coatings, construction
- eg. araldite - first part is the resin, second is the hardener, usually amine based
