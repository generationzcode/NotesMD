* Looking at player 1 here, strategy A strictly dominates strategy 2
* However strategy B over D is weak dominance as every outcome is only greater than or equal to the outcome from the other strategy
* A strategy is a strictly dominant strategy if it strictly dominates every other strategy a player can take
* A strategy is weakly dominant is it weakly dominates or is equal to any other strategy possible for the player
* A strictly dominant profile/equilibrium/solution is when a particular strategy, say (A,F) (its not...) is strictly dominant for every player
* Same for weakly dominant
