* $$H_L(L'-L) = H_V(-F+L-L')+FH_F$$

* => $$\frac{L'-L}{F} = \frac{H_F-H_V}{H_L-H_V} = q$$
* q is the fraction of liquid in feed OR heat required to vaporize feed divided by heat of vaporization
