> High impact Polystyrene

![[Pasted image 20250130145032.png]]
- A graft copolymer of polystyrene and SBR (styrene butadiene rubber)
- [[SBR]] is also a copolymer
- better impact strength than polystyrene
- SBR rubber improves the impact strength
- used in home appliances
 ![[Pasted image 20250130145322.png]]
- unit of impact strength is J/mm
- when you are breaking the specimen, you cause crack propagation, the rubber absorbs the crack energy
- 1.03 g/cm3
- no standard grafting percentages
- cannot graft beyond 20-30 percent 
- translucent not transparent
