## Utility function
- $$u(x_1,x_2) = x_1^\alpha x_2^{1-\alpha}$$
- The above is the cobb douglas utility
- Cobb-Douglas demand:
- $$x_1 = \frac{\alpha m}{p_1}$$
- Clearly linear
## Production function
- $$f(x_1,x_2) = Ax_1^\alpha x_2^{1-\alpha}$$
- The A is important since magnitudes matter in the production function
