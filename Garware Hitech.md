### Paint protection films
These are films people put on their cars to protect them from scratches. Here, Garware is competing with 3M, a massive, massive company in polymers similar to [[Supreme Industries]]
 PPFs are self healing and leaving them out in the sun can heal them up. Theres a lot of research going on in this area - Self healing polymers (pioneered by white)

80% of PPFs are being exported.
PPF is *reported* to now make up 30% of garware's total revenue in just 4 years after launch

Very few companies manufacture this so little competition
### Automotive films
These are car window films
Mostly used to keep out the sun from your car, using light reflective, infrared reflective, metallized, tinted films
__They optimize energy efficiency__
They shrink fit so it's relatively easy application, they help reduce window scratches and are compliant with the law
### Architectural films
These are to reduce AC costs in buildings without ruining the view, by reducing the glare and UV rays. Also find use in the fact that the films protect the people inside the buildings from the glass breaking and injuring them
Privacy films are also used in cabins, important rooms
Decorative films for malls, hotels, airports and such areas

## Industrial products division
