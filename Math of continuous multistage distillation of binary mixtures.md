### Envelope $E_1$
* mB => $$V=L+D$$
* CB => $$Vy_1 = Lx_1+Dx_D$$
* EB => $$VH_v = LH_L+DH_D+Q_C$$
### envelope $E_2$
* mB => $$V+RD=D(1-R)+L$$
* CB => $$Vy_1+DRx_D = D(1-R)x_D+Lx_1$$
* EB => $$VH_{V,1}+DRH_D = D(1-R)H_D+LH_{L,1}$$
### operating line for rectification section
$$y_{n+1} = \frac{R}{R+1}x_n+\frac{1}{1+R}x_D$$
### Operating line for stripping section when the reboiler ratio isn't given
$$y_{n-1} = \frac{L'}{L'-W}x_n-\frac{W}{L'-W}x_W$$
## Rectification section
* flow rates in the rectifying section are called L or V for liquid or vapour
## Stripping section
* flow rates of stuff going up and down in the stripping section are L' and V' for liquid and vapour
## Number of trays
* graphical solution of material balance equations together with the equilibrium relation
* constant molar overflow of liquid and vapour from one tray to another over any section of the distillation column
* How to do it:
>[!todo] Steps!
>1. Define the degree of separation for a given feed flow composition
>2. Assume reflux ratio(if not given)
>3. Calculate top and bottom flow rates by overall material balance
>4. Calculate the $P^{sat}$ values for each component
>5. Calculate equilibrium x-y data from the $P^{sat}$ values using Raoults law
>6. draw equilibrium curve using x-y data
>7. Locate $z_F$,$x_D$ and $x_W$ on the diagonal of the x-y curve
>8. Calculate and draw operating lines for the rectifying and stripping sections
>9. draw steps between equilibrium curve and the operating lines to find out the number of ideal stages. Construction may start from either $x_D$ or $x_W$
>10. when the feed line is crossed, do crossover from one operating line to the other (a transition from rectification to stripping line)
