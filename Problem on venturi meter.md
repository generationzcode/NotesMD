
>[!Venturi problem]
>person A doesnt want to eat mustard sauce at the rate of 11LPS. We will neverthless force him. He uses a lawn hose connected to a centrifugal pump. Lawn hose pipe diameter is 4cm, throat diameter is 1cm, C_o is 0.98 for venturi meter to make sure the flow rate is 11LPS. density of mustard sauce is 1000kg/m3. Whats the pressure difference expected? Forgive my poor drawing skills
>![[Pasted image 20240718123151.png]]

### Solution
$$0.011 = 0.98*\pi/4*1^2\sqrt{\frac{2(\Delta P)}{1000-1000(\frac{1}{4})^4}}$$
Putting this in equation solver:
> The pressure drop is 1017 pascal for agastya to eat 11 litres of mustard sauce every second
