## Why are Tyres Black?
* they are black because of carbon black which is added in the rubber
* the black causes a higher frictional factor
* the carbon black is very conductive - its good at dissipating the frictional (adiabatic) heat that is transferred into the tyre
* the black causes good weather resistance - weatherability. Good resistance toward UV
## Dyes vs Pigments
* Dyes are medium (example: water) soluble, pigments are not soluble in mediums
* Pigments are used in polymer engineering
## Dispersions vs emulsions vs solutions
* solutions cause the solute and solvent to be in a single phase
* dispersion is a state of mixture when the substances won't settle or float(go up or down)
* emulsion is a state of mixture when it's added binary phase but becomes single phase with surface tension effects

## Viscosity
* Viscosity is the resistance to flow
* Materials that thin when you shear them, are non newtonian (viscosity reduces with shear). Polymers are non newtonian
* Since they thin when we add shear, it's more processable
* it's a function of temperature and pressure. Any change in either of these variables will cause a change in viscosity

## Classification of Polymers
![[Pasted image 20240714153112.png]]
* The classification most people use when speaking about polymers is of them divided into - thermoplastics, elastomers and thermosets
* [[Thermoplastics]] are linear of branched polymers which become liquid upon application of heat. They can be moulded and remoulded into any shape. They do not crystallise easily. Many of these are amorphous - not crystalline - and are characterized by their glass transition temperature, at which they move between glassy and rubbery states. **Can be remoulded multiple times**
* [[Elastomers]] are crosslinked polymers that are rubbery. They aren't called rubber since that word is for rubbery polymers that arent crosslinked. Elastomers are characterised by their capability to be stretched into large lengths and return to their original state. These are thermoplastics
* [[Thermoset]] polymers are polymers that cant be remoulded or melted. They have very high degrees of crosslinking. Eg rubber after vulcanisation
