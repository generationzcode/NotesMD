## Process
- plastic melted, formed into a hollow tube about a "blow stick"
- mold is placed about the blow stick as shown above
- the plastic is blown *out of proportion*
- plastic cools on the shape of the mold
- mold opens, product is ejected
## Extrusion Blow Molding
- Continuous hollow tube (parison) extruded vertically
## Injection Blow Molding
- parison injected
