## [[CSTR]] (pronounced c-star)
* This lovely little lump of metal has an inlet, an outlet and a tank with an agitator
* The tank is kept at a lower concentration of the reactant than the inlet fluid has
* The liquid in the tank is completely homogenous (fancy word for same throughout)
* This is also called the backmix reactor and I personally prefer calling it the backmix reactor
* Space time for the reactor is $V/v$ where V is the reactor volume and $v$ is the inlet flow rate
* $r_{A} = -kC_{A}$ is the rate for first order, $C_{A}$ is constant throughout, so the rate of reaction is also constant
* Expansion, also known as -redacted-, is accounted for in the concentrations only, doesn't affect much else
