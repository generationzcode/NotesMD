* Books - Levenspiel, Fogler, Kirk Othmer encyclopedia of chemical technology, Ullmans encyclopedia of industrial chemistry, Mcketta (some encyclopedia on industrial chemistry)
* Course content:
* Raw material and energy sources
* Organic and inorganic intermediates and final products
* Bulk and specialty chemicals
* Production costs of fuel and chemicals
* Industrial gases and inorganic products
* Examples of major industrial processes - hand in hand with the last point
* Types of chemical reactions : elementary and non elementary, single and multiple, reversible and irreversible
* Types of chemical reactors : Batch and semi batch, continuous reactors ( CSTR anf PFR)
* Reaction kinetics (homogeneous reactions)
* Isothermal, adiabatic and non isothermal operation modes
* Different types of single phase and multiphase reactors
