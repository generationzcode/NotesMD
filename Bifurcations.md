## Transcritical Bifurcation

## Pitchfork bifurcation
- Saddle and node, except stuff disappears in pairs and with problems that have **symmetry**
- A good way to visualize this is to imagine a beam with a weight above it. If the weight exceeds a certain amount, the beam will buckle. But there's no telling which way it will buckle. So there are really 3 fixed points, just two occur together and one occurs by itself. One is when the beam doesn't buckle and the other two are when the beam buckles on either side
- Two types of bifurcation
