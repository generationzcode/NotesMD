### What do you mean "ripening cheese"?
- depending on how long you ferment it - you can get soft, semi soft and hard cheese
- The ripening of cheese involves:
#### Glycolysis
- lactose is broken down to lactic acid, which may be catabolized (broken down) to form acetic acid, propionic acid, carbon dioxide, esters and alcohol
- ![[Pasted image 20250710111136.png]]
#### Lipolysis
- lipids in milk are broken down to form free fatty acids that may be catabolised to form ketones, lactones and esters by rennet enzymes
#### Proteolysis
- proteins are broken down to form peptides and amino acids by the enzymes of the coagulant, the natural milk enzymes and the enzymes of the starter bacteria
